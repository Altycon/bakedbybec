Do you have gluten free products?

Our kitchen often uses flour, butter, sugar, eggs, milk, peanuts and/or tree nuts. Extreme effort is put into preventing contamination on work surfaces, but it may happen. If you are concerned about severe allergens, I strongly advise against ordering at this time.
