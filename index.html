Do you have gluten free products?

No, I do not offer gluten-free products at this time. My flour contains wheat grain which means cakes and cookies are not suitable for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

Ingredients and Food Allergens

Our kitchen often uses flour, butter, sugar, eggs, milk, peanuts and/or tree nuts. Extreme effort is put into preventing contamination on work surfaces, but it may happen.

If you are concerned about severe allergens, I strongly advise against ordering at this time.
