I will travel to your venue of choice, and bring all decorating materials necessary for you and your guests. After 30 minutes of prep time before your class, each cookie station will be ready to go! Classes are scheduled between 1.5-2 hours in length giving plenty of time for decorating, questions, and conversation.

Tables and chairs will need to be provided by either you or your venue. Minimum of 6 people in attendance per class.
