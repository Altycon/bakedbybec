options

    $25/child

    Kids with a flair for creating or baking? Let them explore and find new skills with a cookie decorating class. Perfect for birthday parties, kids are sure to have a blast learning something new!

  • Adults

    $35/person

    Girls night out? Date night? Work party? Join me and explore your creative side with cookie decorating. No theme is off-limits! Classes range from beginner to advanced, so all levels are welcome!

  • Decorating Duos

    $35/adult & 1 child, $10/additional child

    Enjoy some unique bonding time with your child and create your very own matching sets of cookies. Instruction is focused on the basics of decorating and just having fun!

  • Airbrushing
  • Watercolor

  • Doodling

details

  • prep time

    30 min

  • class length

    1.5 - 2 hours

  • materials

    provided

I will be traveling to your venue and be bring all the materials you and your guests will need!

I need only 30 minutes for setup and to prepare each cookie station.

I only require a table and chairs (provided by you or the venue) for your guests to sit at and to prepare for setup.

My class is scheduled for 1.5 - 2 hours in length.

process

During the class I guide each guest individually and the group as a whole through each step of the decorating process.

Along the way you will obtain basic knowledge of:

  • icing consistencies
  • piping techniques
  • baking tips
  • cookie recipes

Each guest creates and decorates 6 sugar cookies of their own!
