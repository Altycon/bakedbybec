In the workshop, you'll learn the basics (yes, it's beginner friendly!) of decorating including: how to keep icing from dripping over the sides of your cookie, how to make different designs pop, icing consistency, and more.

All supplies are included! I provide the cookies, icing, practice sheets, recipe cards, and decorating tools; you bring yourself (and a friend!) If you want to take your skills home, you can purchase an add-on kit to take with you after the class.

If you're interested in a class, send me your email below and I'll respond with more information.
