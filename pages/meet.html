meet  the
Baker

I'm Bec, Head Baker of Baked by Bec. I am located in West Point, Iowa. If you've never heard of that, it's just a small drive-thru town in the middle of nowhere üòÇ Seriously though, the community is amazing! Probably some of the nicest people you'll meet.

Baked by Bec started in 2021. I began baking because I've always enjoyed exploring the creative side of my mind. Baking and decorating cookies started out as a hobby I would dabble in every once in a while, but it really became something I loved, and still love to do. Taking your words and ideas and making them into an edible creation brings me joy.

Sugar cookies are the specialty dessert of Baked by Bec. There's a cookie for every occasion! I've done birthday parties, graduations, retirements, work parties, and just-because events! There's really no end to the list of reasons you need cookies. Head on over to the creations page to take a peek at some of my favorites! In addition to cookies, there are many other sweet options available including: cakes, cupcakes, and cake pops. Each dessert is open to complete design customization.

When I'm not baking, I'm a teacher and I spend my days teaching some of the most amazing kids! The rest of my time is spent soaking up every fleeting moment with my two daughters, working on new designs and recipes for my desserts, playing piano, and listening to music.
