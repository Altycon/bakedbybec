Meet the Baker

I’m Bec, Head Baker of Baked by Bec. I am located in West Point, Iowa. If you’ve never heard of that, it’s just a small drive-thru town in the middle of nowhere 😂 Seriously though, the community is amazing! Probably some of the nicest people you’ll meet live here.

Baked by Bec started a little over a year ago officially. I began by giving away cookies for free to family and friends but I really hadn't thought of turning this hobby into a business until my good friend Elise encouraged me. So, Elise, this goes out to you.🥂

Baked by Bec specializes in custom sugar cookies, for every occasion! I’ve done birthday parties, graduations, retirements, work parties, and just-because events! There’s really no end to the list of reasons you need cookies 😂

When I’m not baking, I’m spending my days teaching some of the most amazing kids! I spend my evenings with my own two daughters , soaking up every fleeting moment with them ♥️

Thanks for sticking around till the end, and I look forward to baking for all of you soon!!
